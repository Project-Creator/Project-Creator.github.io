Strike Force Heros 2

Basic Info

In Strike Force Heros 2, you will battle other players or bots in an epic flash game that can captivate in a second. Buy weapons, upgrade, and get more skines to dominate the battlefield. An important part of this game is campain mode, wich goes along with strike force heros 1.

Some Things You Need to Know

  • Using the slot machine to get more weapons is recommended.
  • Your not fighting players in campain mode, just bots.
  • You can also buy weapons.
